Background and Motivation for Engineering

I moved to Australia from Vietnam in 2017, driven by my family’s sacrifices and inspired by the potential of engineering to shape the future. As a Mechatronic Engineer, I’m passionate about robotics, automation, and system design.

Through representing my family business at AB Relation PTY LTD, and previously working at OPTIK CONSULTANCY and WINDTECH CONSULTANCY as an intern, I’ve developed strong teamwork, empathy, and problem-solving skills. Explore my portfolio to discover my journey, skills, and innovative projects.

My experiences at AB Relation PTY LTD, OPTIK CONSULTANCY, and WINDTECH CONSULTANCY as an intern have shaped me into a dynamic and adaptable professional.

Working with diverse teams, I’ve honed my ability to navigate challenges with positivity, flexibility, and an open mind, always focused on achieving objectives. These roles have strengthened my skills in connecting with people, understanding their unique perspectives, and contributing to shared success.
